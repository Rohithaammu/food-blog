Delicious Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g Pasta
  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 cup Tomato Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water for 8-10 minutes until al dente.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce and season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Combine the pasta and sauce. Stir well and serve hot.
Tiffins Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Semolina (Rava)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1/4 cup Water
  • 1 tablespoon Oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon Mustard Seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped vegetables (Carrot, Beans, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add turmeric powder and chopped vegetables. Cook for 5 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, mix semolina, yogurt, and water to form a batter.
  4. Pour the batter into the pan and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally until it sets.
  5. Serve hot with chutney or sambar.
Homemade Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Flour
  • 1 tablespoon Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 tablespoons Melted Butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine both mixtures and stir until smooth.
  4. Heat a pan and cook pancakes until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve with syrup, fruits, or your favorite toppings.
Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 1/2 kg Chicken (or your preferred meat)
  • 1 Onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Yogurt
  • 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Garam Masala
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 2 Cinnamon Sticks
  • 2 Cloves
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice until 70% done, set aside.
  2. In a large pan, sauté onions until golden brown.
  3. Add chicken and spices, cook until chicken is tender.
  4. Layer the rice on top of the chicken and let it cook for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with raita or salad.
